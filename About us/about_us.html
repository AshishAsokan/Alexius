About Us

We,Alexius, are a crowd sourced review system. Our primary goal is to help you make more informed decisions when it comes to almost everything. Whether it's deciding on a restaurant to catch up with your friends, or deciding on a school for your little ones, we got you covered. Our verified user system helps in increasing the reliability and standard of the reviews on the website.

You are also free to express your opinions yourself in the form of reviews and contribute to the Alexius community. For any further queries or feedback, please feel free to visit the 'Contact Us' page. We'd love to hear from you!
